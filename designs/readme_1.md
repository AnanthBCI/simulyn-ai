Simulyn AI Dashboard — UI/UX Improvement Notes
Overview

This document summarizes the UI/UX improvements made from the original Simulyn AI dashboard design to the updated enterprise-focused version.

The goal of the redesign was to transform the product from a:

Construction Monitoring Dashboard

into an:

AI Construction Decision Platform

The redesign focused on:

Better decision visibility
Stronger AI positioning
Faster risk identification
Executive-level dashboard experience
Improved business value communication
Major Improvements Implemented
1. Critical Alert Visibility
Old Design
Risks were hidden inside widgets and charts.
Users needed to scan the screen to understand urgent issues.
New Design

Added a dedicated high-priority alert banner at the top of the dashboard.

Example:
1 CRITICAL DELAY RISK DETECTED
Expected Delay: 10 Days
Potential Cost Impact: $18,600
Recommended Action: Add 2 workers
Benefits
Immediate attention to urgent issues
Faster executive decision-making
Strong enterprise dashboard feel
2. AI Recommendation Clarity
Old Design
AI insights existed but looked passive.
Recommendations were mixed with generic information.
New Design

Added a dedicated:

AI Recommended Actions

panel.

Example Recommendations
Add workers to slab concreting
Reschedule plumbing tasks
Increase concrete crew
Monitor weather impact
Benefits
Makes AI actionable
Positions product as decision assistant
Stronger differentiation from competitors
